Classic Cookies!

Description

Another classic recipe in our household. They're printed on the back of a bag I think - everyone raves though. *shrug*

Ingredients

  • 1 c. Softened Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 c. Sugar
  • 1 1/2 c. Packed Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 1/2 tsp. Vanilla
  • 3 c. Flour
  • 3/4 tsp. Salt
  • 1 tsp. Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp. Baking Powder
  • 12 oz. Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips (1 bag)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees fan.
  2. In Sir Mix a Lot (Kitchen Aid mixing bowl), cream the butter for 2-3 minutes on medium high speed.
  3. Add sugars, cream another 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, cream for a final 1-2 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, salt, baking soda and baking powder.
  6. Pour the dry ingredients into the bowl with the mixer
  7. Stir in chocolate chips
  8. Place golf-ball size portions 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.
  9. Bake for 10 1/2 minutes or until edges are just starting to turn brown.
